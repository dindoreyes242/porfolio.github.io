About Tata Nuñez:

I am Ana Cheryl “Tata” Nuñez- Castro. I am the 2nd daughter of Cateel Mayor Erlinda Calañas Nuñez and the late Vice Mayor, Camilo “Miloy” Toroba Nuñez. Like most of you, our family lived an impoverished life. Looking back, we went through all the struggles that throughout our lives we will never forget- the need to live to a relative’s house, hungry stomach, name it all. In the hope of giving hope to every story similar to ours, our journey in politics started. Aside from my father, who also served as Mayor in my hometown, I was also deeply inspired by my Aunt, the current 1st District Representative of Davao Oriental, Corazon Toroba Nuñez-Malanyaon to enter into politics. I first worked for her as Social Services Staff. Then, in 2007, I was elected as Brgy Kagawad for 6 years (2 terms). Consequently, I was elected as Municipal Councilor from 2013- up to present. During my term as SB Member, we have reached out to thousand of families and provided assistance in the best way we could. We still have a long way to go. So, please, allow me touch more lives and together, let’s bring hope to stories same as mine as I aspire to be a Board Member of the 1st District of Davao Oriental.
